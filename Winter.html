Winter plants - Pansy


What is pansy?


The garden pansy is a type of large-flowered hybrid plant cultivated as a garden flower.
It is derived by hybridization from several species in the section Melanium of the genus Viola, particularly V. tricolor,
a wildflower of Europe and western Asia known as heartsease
